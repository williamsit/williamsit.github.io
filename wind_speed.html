Wind Speed vs Latitude

Summary: In this project, I gathered weather data from over 600 different cities. First, I used Python (Pandas and Matplotlib) and OpenWeather API in order to grab the proper data on cities that were randomly generated. Then, I created a series of scatter plots in order to showcase the relationship between different factors (such as Wind Speed and Percentage of Cloudiness) against the cities' Latitude and Longitude.
