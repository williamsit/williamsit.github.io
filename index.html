Weather Py Project

Created by William Sit

Introduction: In this project, I wanted to see if there were any factors that affect a given city's weather forecast. I decided to divide my analysis on four specific factors:

  • Maximum Temperature
  • Wind Speed
  • Humidity
  • Cloudiness
I gathered weather data from over 600 different cities. First, I used Python (Pandas and Matplotlib) and Open Weather API in order to grab the proper data on cities that were randomly generated. Then, I created a series of scatter plots in order to showcase the relationship between different factors (such as Wind Speed and Percentage of Cloudiness) against the cities' Latitude.
